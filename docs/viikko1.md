# RoomLight – Smart Hotel Lighting. Perfect once – sync everywhere

**LAB Lighting Oy** announced today the official launch of **RoomLight**, a next generation hotel room lighting controller that allows hotels to design the perfect lighting setup once and instantly sync it to every room in hotel.

Every hotel manager knows the problem: inconsistent lighting, hours spent manually configurating dozens of lights across hundreds of different rooms and areas.

**RoomLight eliminates the problem!**

> ”We wanted to create something that simplifyes hotel management while improving guest comfort. Lighting should enhance the hotel experience rather than cause extra work.” Said a Product Engineer at LAB Lightning Oy.

With a simple installation. RoomLight is connected to the hotels’s lighting system – and then it can be managed through a simple software – that allows you to create lighting profiles, sync them across all desired rooms, set timed lightings for different occasions and easily update plans without physical room visits.

> ”Before RoomLight, adjusting lighting across our hotel took hours. Now it takes minutes! Our rooms feel consistent and professionally designed” Said a hotel operations manager after a three-month pilot period.

# FAQ

## How many rooms can RoomLight control?

There is no set limit. Even the biggest hotels can be handled with our system.

## Does every room need to be configured separately?

No. Lighting is designed once and can be synced instantly to every room.

## Can lighting profiles be customized for different room types?

Yes. Normal rooms, suites, halls, restaurant, you name it. Every room can have it’s own lighting. You can even set different mood with lighting for breakfast and dinner times for example.

## Can guests still control the lights manually?

Yes. Guest can adjust the settings as they wish with limiations. Once the guest’s hotel visit is over the lighting can be resetted back to hotel’s standard lighting.
